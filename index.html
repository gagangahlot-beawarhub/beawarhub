About Beawar, Rajasthan

Beawar is a vibrant city located in the Ajmer district of Rajasthan. Known for its rich heritage, bustling markets, and cultural harmony, Beawar is a hub for trade and tourism. Popular areas include Ajmer Road, Chavni, Dungri, Mevadi Gate, and Surajpol. With landmarks like Prabhu Ki Bagiya and local bazaars full of color and energy, Beawar offers a unique blend of tradition and progress. BeawarHub is your digital gateway to this beautiful city.
